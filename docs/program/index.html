Program

alwd2017-program.pdf

The program committee is pleased to share with you the outstanding lineup of speakers for the 2017 ALWD Conference. The program will run from 2:00 on Wednesday, July 19 through 4:00 on Friday, July 21.

Please plan to join us for an opening reception and thought-provoking play on Wednesday at 5:30, an awards presentation on Thursday during the lunch hour, and a visit to the museum and gala dinner on Thursday at 5:00. 

Plan to stay through Friday evening to find an adventure or just a quiet dinner with friends.

We are so thankful to all of our sponsors for helping us to put on this outstanding program. Please look for a detailed program with our list of thank-yous at the conference.
